During National Education Week..

The A.J.W.M.S. Multidimensional High School in Raozan Gahira, located near the Chittagong-Rangamati highway, is the center of everyone's attention for its scenic beauty. Established in 1930, the school achieved excellence during the National Education Week in 2017. This school is managed on par with renowned city schools. The students and teachers of the school are enjoying all facilities and opportunities.

It is known that, due to the insight of the area's Member of Parliament and the Chairman of the Parliamentary Standing Committee on Railways Ministry, Mr. A.B.M. Fazle Karim Chowdhury, various infrastructures of the school, including the educational environment, have been upgraded. The school now features modern computer labs, two multimedia classrooms, closed-circuit cameras for continuous monitoring in each class and outside the school, modern toilet facilities, two science laboratories, trained Girls Guides, a Scout troop, a well-equipped library, a branch of the World Literature Center, various development-oriented display boards, two large gates, a spacious hall with climate control, a swimming pool, and a large park with a flourishing garden..

Nostalgic Remarks From Former Student.

In the warm embrace of nostalgia, the 2014 reunion at Gahira A.J.W.M.S. Multidimensional High School was a heartwarming journey down memory lane. Alumni from various batches gathered with smiles and excitement, ready to reconnect with old friends and relive cherished moments.

The school grounds echoed with laughter and joy as former students explored the familiar corridors, classrooms, and the iconic landmarks that shaped their academic years. The air was filled with stories of shared experiences, unforgettable teachers, and the transformative journey each had taken since graduating.
