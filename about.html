About Us

This prestigious school in the heart of Raozan Police Station in Chittagong is located in North Chittagong. Established in 1930, the school was founded through the initiative of the local youth society in the area, on land donated by the affluent local figure Abdul Jabbar Sahib.From the 6th to the 10th grade, approximately 900 students are studying in 5 classes and 13 sections in this school. The school consistently performs well in terms of results. Every time, 75% of the students pass the secondary examination. The school is meeting the educational needs of the general boys and girls in the surrounding area at a low cost.

Mission

M

The Alumni Association of our prestigious school, nestled in the heart of Raozan Police Station, Chittagong, is committed to fostering a vibrant community of graduates who are not only connected by shared experiences but also driven by a collective mission. Our mission is to uphold the legacy of academic excellence, character development, and community service that has defined our alma mater for decades. Through collaborative efforts, we aim to contribute to the growth and prosperity of both our alumni and the school.

Vision

V

Our vision is to create a dynamic and engaged network of alumni who serve as pillars of support for each other and for the institution. We aspire to build bridges that span generations, enabling the exchange of knowledge, resources, and opportunities. With a forward-looking perspective, we envision our alumni playing pivotal roles in shaping a brighter future for themselves, the community, and the school. Through our collective impact, we aim to be a source of inspiration for current students, encouraging them to strive for excellence in all facets of life..

History

H

Over the years, our school has consistently achieved outstanding results, with an impressive 75% of students passing the secondary examination each time. Beyond academic success, the institution has played a crucial role in meeting the educational needs of the general population in the surrounding area, offering quality education at an affordable cost. As alumni, we take pride in our shared history and the values instilled in us during our time at this esteemed institution. It is with a sense of gratitude and responsibility that we come together to form the Alumni Association.
